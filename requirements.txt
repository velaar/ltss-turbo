## Requirements

- Home Assistant 2023.1 or newer
- PostgreSQL 12+ 
- Python 3.9+

### Optional PostgreSQL Extensions

- **TimescaleDB** (strongly recommended for compression and hypertables)
- **PostGIS** (optional, enables GPS/location tracking)

> **Note**: LTSS Turbo works without TimescaleDB or PostGIS, but you'll get significantly better performance and compression with TimescaleDB enabled.
